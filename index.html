About Us

DogTown is a company dedicated to ensuring all dogs, regardless of their owner's current activity level, due to time constrictions, health issues, etc., get to live an active, healthy lifestyle. By pairing people who sometimes do not have time to spend with their pets with individuals who are willing to pick up the animal and take them on hikes, walks, jogs, camping trips, etc., we can assist in giving the dog the life it deserves, as well as peace of mind for the owner, knowing their dog is being taken care and getting enough exercise to remain healthy when their owner cannot do it personally.


Perhaps you want your dog to help someone such as lending your dog to a medical facility or college campus as a therapy dog. This is something that has proven to be popular in recent years and is another great option for this app. We are very confident that by using this app, we can help create a better life for dogs and people alike.
